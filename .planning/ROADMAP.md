# Roadmap: Antler Room Website

**Created:** 2026-01-19
**Core Value:** Artists can discover Antler Room's credibility through their track record and submit finished tracks through a professional, on-brand experience.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation & Hero
**Goal:** Establish project structure, base styling, navigation, and hero section
**Plans:** 2 plans

**Requirements:**
- NAV-01, NAV-02, NAV-03
- HERO-01, HERO-02, HERO-03, HERO-04
- VIS-01, VIS-02, VIS-03, VIS-04

**Success Criteria:**
1. User lands on page and sees ANTLER ROOM hero with dark background and gold accents
2. Navigation is visible and clicking links scrolls smoothly to sections (placeholders)
3. "SUBMIT DEMOS" CTA is prominent in nav
4. Typography follows brand guidelines (all-caps headers, clean body text)

### Phase 3: Polish & Responsive
**Goal:** Add visual polish (textures, motifs) and ensure responsive design across devices

**Requirements:**
- NAV-04
- VIS-05, VIS-06, VIS-07
- RESP-01, RESP-02, RESP-03, RESP-04

**Success Criteria:**
1. Mobile hamburger menu works correctly
2. Sunburst dividers appear between sections
3. Grain texture overlay creates lo-fi aesthetic
4. Site looks good on mobile (320px), tablet (768px), and desktop (1200px+)
5. All tap targets are appropriately sized on mobile
